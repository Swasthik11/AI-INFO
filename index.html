AI plays a significant role in our daily lives

The goal of AI is to create systems that can operate autonomously, making decisions and taking actions on their own without the need for direct human input.

Machine Learning

The ability of AI systems to learn from data and improve their performance.

Computer Vision

The ability to process and analyze visual information, such as images and videos.

Natural Language

The ability of AI systems to interpret, and generate human language.

Robotics

The development of autonomous robots that can perform tasks with human interaction.
